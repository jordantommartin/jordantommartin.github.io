Hello. I'm Jordan, a goal-driven game programmer focused on creating exciting moments from complex problems. I enjoy working with teams and learning the intricate details of each project I work on.

Currently, I am studying at the Florida Interactive Entertainment Academy and working on delivering Zenko: A Fox's Tale to steam in August 2021. I have been at FIEA since Fall of 2020, working on a variety of projects including Rapid Prototypes, a Custom Game Engine written in C++, and other games using OpenGL. Before coming to FIEA, I worked for a defense company where I administrated company-wide Atlassian and Open-source web applications to a user base of nearly 5000 people.

Additionally, I have helped develop projects invovling Natural Language Generation and Distributed Simulation for the University of Central Florida and NASA. I am graduating in the Fall of 2021 and would welcome the chance to speak about game programming opportunities. Thank you for your time.
