Balloon Fight

Progamming 1 Individual Project / OpenGL and C++

Balloon Fight was a clone I made for our final project in our Programming 1 class. Created in C++ and integrated with OpenGL, I learned how to do render sprites and animations from a sprite sheet, manage inputs from the keyboard, and create custom player and enemy movement. This clone has you going through waves of enemies instead of completing new levels.
