LOOT

Programming 1 Individual Project / 68K Assembly

LOOT was a game I made for our interactive assembly project in our Programming 1 class. You play as a knight who must collect as many gold coins as he can without touching any of the bouncing fireballs. Created in 68K assembly, I learned to render sprites with limited resources and use tables to store positions of objects in the game.
